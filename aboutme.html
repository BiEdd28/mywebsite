About Me

A brief hsitory of my life.


Childhood

I was born in the year 1994, it was a fresh Sunday evening. Winter had just begun that mid-November in Danli, El Paraiso, Honduras.

I was raised in Honduras from ages 1-14. Considering how long ago this was, there isn't much that I remember. My two younger sisters were born during this time and that changed up my life quite a bit but everything went as you'd expect (attended kindergarten, elementary and middle school). Times did get a little rough when I was 14 and that led my parents to make the decision to travel to the US in hopes to live the "American Dream."

Teen Years
Interesting Experiences

My teen years were very rough considering the drastic transition. Picture this:

  • You left behind all your friends and extended family
  • You don't even know how to begin to grasp English
  • You struggle to keep up with school because of the language barrier
  • Making friends isn't as easy as it used to be because 'English'
  • You face the hard realization that graduation is around the corner when your adult life is about to begin and have no head starts
This was essentially my teenage life but, in the end, I persevered and scraped by just fine.

Adulthood
Work Goals

I really broke out of my shell when I graduated highschool. I set high goals for myself but had to start from square one. In the following 2 years I went through about 8 jobs trying to figure out what I liked and disliked. I realized that I wanted to further my career but had to stick to a single job to save money and do just that. I stuck around for 2 years in DHL (a logistics center) to earn enough and pay through a Medical Insurance Billing and Coding certification but I sadly was not able to land a good job with this and back to the drawing board I went. Given my experience in logistics, I applied to Amazon and have stuck around for 5 years so far. During this time I got to know myself better and decided to learn software programming to eventually make this my career and further myself financially.

Hobbies and Things that I Enjoy

I really enjoy spending time with my family and going outside the city to catch fresh air and a glimpse of the darker skies - this has become my hobby. Every July, I make an effort to visit Pioche, NV to enjoy the latter. Pioche may have a tiny population but it has some amazing night skies.
